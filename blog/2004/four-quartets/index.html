Four Quartets

God, I love this poem.

Time to be honest. I’ve yet to read it. There are just so many excerpts that I find amazing that I’ve never felt the need to actually read it! I think I better soon.

Here’s how it starts:

Time present and time past
Are both perhaps present in time future
And time future contained in time past.
If all time is eternally present
All time is unredeemable.

Wow.

After reading several parts of the poem, I realize that a short story I recently wrote was partially inspired by Eliot. Interesting…
