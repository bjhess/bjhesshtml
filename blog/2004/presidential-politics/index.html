Presidential Politics

I’m having an interesting time trying to sort through my opinions on the presidential candidates. A recent discussion has resulted in my reevaluating exactly what qualities should be important in a president. Though I don’t think it will change who I originally planned on voting for, it will probably help me to feel less horrible if the “wrong” person wins.

There are definitely some things about each candidate I simply wish could go away, but such is life.
