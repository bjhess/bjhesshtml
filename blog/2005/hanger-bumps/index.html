Hanger Bumps

So I watched Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones tonight and saw easily one of my favorite “mistakes” I’ve ever seen in a movie. In one of the scenes of Anakin and Padmé hiding on Naboo, Anakin’s shirt has those annoying hanger bumps on each shoulder. I believe the scene was the one right before the really bad romance scene in the meadow.

Now maybe George Lucas was really going for extreme realism, but somehow I doubt it from a guy who needs to computer animate human footprints into sand. I laughed my butt off. No wonder Anakin is so moody; he’s embarrassed!
