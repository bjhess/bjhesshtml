Weddings

Two of the past three weekends I was attending weddings. One in which I was the best man, and this past weekend when The Wife was matron of honor. So that’s where I’ve been. I’m still tired.

Another great feature of this wedding was the vast participation in the dance. Actually, I don’t think I’ve been to a wedding with this much participation since my own wedding - and this wedding probably had a little more activity. (Well, there was maybe one wedding off-hand that was pretty close.) I don’t dance, but if everyone is out there I’ll be coaxed into a little bit of rug-cutting.
