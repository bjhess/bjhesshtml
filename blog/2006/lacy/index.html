This is an old-school picture of The Little Sister (pre-braces!) and
our former dog, Lacy. Lacy was a dalmation that arrived at the home
of The Parents around the same time as The Little Sister. She grew up
with Lacy.

At the point this picture was taken, or perhaps a little later, Lacy's
skull collapsed on one side. She basically had a dent in one side of
her skull that almost looked like an inverse half of a baseball. I'm
guessing the cause was tumor-related. She deteriorated from there and
eventually her misery was too great.

But I thought this was a cute picture from way back when. Ah, the memories.
