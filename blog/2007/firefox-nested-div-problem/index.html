Firefox Nested Div Problem

I got absolutely nailed by a “bug” in Firefox today. I had a div tag within a div tag, but the outer div tag was not surrounding the content in the inner div tag. Turns out in Firefox divs do not play nicely with content when the content is all block level floating content. Lesson learned.

Thank you, Internets, for solving my problem!
