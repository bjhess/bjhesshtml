Scrawlers Has Launched

It’s my pleasure to announce the public ALPHA release of Scrawlers. ALPHA is not just a clever moniker in this case; Scrawlers will be changing a lot in the coming months. What you see today represents the minimum amount of features we felt were necessary to justify a release.

Please stop in, don’t be afraid to sign up, and you might as well write a story while you’re there. And don’t be a stranger. The true power of Scrawlers will surface when readers give constructive notes to writers. We’d like to foster a writing workshop community where the foremost goal is to help everyone improve.

Let me know what you think. Or let us know what you think on The Scrawl Blog - probably the best place to keep up with the changing state of Scrawlers.

March 9, 2007
