Two Newsletters

Another newsletter? Who needs that! You might not, but I guess I do. And so I have started up a second newsletter, A Good Enough Newsletter with my friend Shawn. We're not entirely sure where this one's going, but it's been fun so far.

Most of you are probably saying, "Barry, how can you have another newsletter when you don't have a first newsletter?" Good question! (Just kidding. That's not a very good question. A cursory look around my site should bring you to my homepage, which should allow you to discover my newsletter. If not, you have an attention-to-detail problem.) My monthly newsletter, March of Time, has been running for a while now, but 2022 found it hitting a good cadence of personal stories mixed with blog content and other discoveries.
