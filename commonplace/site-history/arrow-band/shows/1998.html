Latin Fire:
The Arrow Marching Band returns to a style they haven't experimented with in 5 years, driving latin jazz, in their 1998 show entitled Latin Fire. The show will open featuring the work of jazz legend Arturo Sandaval in their first two selections, A Mis Abuelos and Tres Palabras. The show closes with perhaps the most famous latin jazz piece ever, Malaguena, by Ernesto Lecuona.

As usual, The Arrow Marching Band will push traditional standards of what highschool marching bands attempt, and what they accomplish. And once again, The Arrow Marching Band will be at the forefront, entertaining crowds like no other.

Sep 12 Halftime Show -- Brookings, SD
The Arrow Marching Band traveled to Brookings, SD to play at halftime of the South Dakota State University football game. This performance provided an opportunity for the band to work out nerves that usually appear during the first competitive performance. It was also a great opportunity to perform in front of an unbiased audience.
