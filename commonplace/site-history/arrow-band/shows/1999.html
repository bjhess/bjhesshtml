Channel One Suite:
For 1999 The Arrow Marching Band ushers in a new era with director Dan Stout, and a classic jazz standard, Channel One Suite by legendary drummer Buddy Rich. Combining elements of swing, bop, and straight- forward jazz, Channel One Suite will show off The Arrow Marching Band's technical and expressive musicianship.

As usual, The Arrow Marching Band will push traditional standards of what highschool marching bands attempt, and what they accomplish. And once again, The Arrow Marching Band will be at the forefront, entertaining audiences like no other.
