I am BARRY HESS

I am one Barry Hess. It turns out there are many in this world, and I am one. Minnesota is where I have resided for most of my life, and it is probably where I'll be for most of the rest of my life.

I try to write every day. My writing can be found on my blog, secret poetry blog, microblog, newsletter, journal entries, and sometimes in the logs of doevery.day. I also keep a commonplace book for my own amusement.

On most days I work a little on internet madness. Sometimes I'll take a break from writing and interneting to do a little woodworking. Years ago I helped build Harvest, and I've always been one to goof off on the web.
